Mission & Vision

SafeSpace is designed to empower communities by providing simple, responsive tools for reporting harassment and requesting help. This front-end demo is built using HTML, CSS & JS only (no backend).

Mission

To offer a quick, accessible way for users to signal danger and report incidents privately and rapidly.

Vision

A world where everyone can move without fear — supported by community-driven safety tools.

Privacy
